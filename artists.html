Our studio is home to some of the industry's most dedicated professionals. Each of our artists is APP-certified and undergoes continuous training in aseptic techniques, anatomical mapping, and jewelry curation.

Senior Piercer

Jax Sterling

With over a decade of experience in high-end modification, Jax is our lead curation specialist. His approach to body art is clinical in its precision but poetic in its execution. Jax specializes in "ear architecture," a method he developed that treats the ear as a multi-dimensional space for bespoke jewelry projects.

After studying aseptic nursing protocols, Jax transitioned into the piercing industry with a mission to eliminate the "living room shop" mentality. He has been a featured speaker at the Association of Professional Piercers (APP) conference and has consulted for major jewelry manufacturers on biocompatible alloy development.

"My goal is to find the hidden geometry within your anatomy and accentuate it with the world's finest metals."

  • Anatomical Mapping

Facial Specialist

Luna Vane

Luna is widely considered the valley's foremost expert in facial symmetry. Her work balances the edgy nature of body modification with a sophisticated, minimalist aesthetic. She believes that a facial piercing should never "interrupt" a face, but rather serve as a focal point that brings out the wearer's natural beauty.

Before joining Ethereal Ink, Luna worked in the high-fashion industry as an editorial stylist. This unique background allows her to offer unparalleled jewelry matching services, helping clients select stones and metals that complement their skin tone and personal style.

"Symmetry is an illusion. My job is to create the perfect visual balance that feels natural to your movements."

Specialties

  • Septum Precision
  • Paired Nostrils
  • Jewelry Styling

Experience

  • 6+ Years Clinical Exp.
  • Advanced Anatomy Cert.

Surface Master

Mika Thorne

Mika is our go-to expert for unconventional body placements. Her technical mastery of dermal anchors and surface bars has earned her a reputation among the city's alternative community. With a background in mechanical engineering, she understands the physical forces—tension, compression, and torque—that act on jewelry in the skin.

Mika is also our lead aftercare researcher. She manages our ongoing clinical study on the efficacy of various wound-care solutions, ensuring that Ethereal Ink always stays at the cutting edge of healing technology. If you have a difficult piercing that won't settle, Mika is the "fixer" you need.

"The body is a machine that wants to heal. I just provide the perfect environment for it to do so."

Specialties

  • Dermal Anchors
  • Surface Bars
  • Migration Repair

Experience

  • 12+ Years Industry Exp.
  • Licensed Body Modifier
